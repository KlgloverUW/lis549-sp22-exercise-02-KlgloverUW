Description

The Camargue is a beautiful natural area in southern France. It has wild ponies. It is near the town where Vincent Van Gogh painted Starry Night.

One fun thing to do in Camargue is to walk on nature trails and view the wildlife. Another fun thing to do is eat a picnic of bread and cheese in one of the lovely green fields.

Directions

From Paris

Take a train south from the station. Ride it several hours. Enjoy looking out the window at the city and then the countryside.

From Nice

Take a bus to Marseille. Then take a train to Arles. From Arles, find a taxi to drive you to the Camargue.
